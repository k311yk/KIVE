Kunst Hive: We are an LA-based creative community space.

A dynamic and innovative space designed to foster a sense of community among creatives. Our hive is committed to organizing regular, recurring daytime workshops and evening events in efforts to cultivate a collective body within the LA area. Each curated showcase highlights local creatives and celebrates art with a focus on face-to-face conversation. KIVE offers a unique opportunity for individuals to gather, connect, and collaborate in an intimate atmosphere stimulating the exchange of knowledge, art and experiences. This space is yours. This space is for everyone.
